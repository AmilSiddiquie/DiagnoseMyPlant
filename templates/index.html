About the Project

Deep Learning meets
Field Agriculture

DiagnoseMyPlant is a deep learning-based plant disease detection system built to give farmers, agronomists, and researchers instant diagnostics.

The model is a custom CNN trained on the PlantVillage dataset. Built with TensorFlow/Keras and served via Flask.

🔍 Currently Supported Crops

Currently, our model is trained to successfully detect 39 distinct diseases across the 14 plants listed below.

  • 🍎 Apple
  • 🫐 Blueberry
  • 🍒 Cherry
  • 🌽 Corn
  • 🍇 Grape
  • 🍊 Orange
  • 🍑 Peach
  • 🫑 Pepper
  • 🥔 Potato
  • 🍇 Raspberry
  • 🌱 Soybean
  • 🎃 Squash
  • 🍓 Strawberry
  • 🍅 Tomato
